About Me

Hi! I'm MD Abdul Kaium, a passionate and dedicated tech enthusiast from Mathbaria, Barisal, Bangladesh. I’m currently pursuing my BSc in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Global Village (UGV), Barisal, and also studying M.A. Kamil (Master's) in Hadith at Jhalokathi N.S. Kamil Madrasah.

I’m a professional Android app developer, IT expert, and the founder & CEO of GoodBuyBangladesh — an online sneaker shop bringing trendy and high-quality sneakers to fashion lovers across the country. I love working with computers, especially in the fields of cybersecurity, Android development, and programming. I believe in challenges, discipline, and building a career based on passion and commitment. My dream is to contribute to the tech world while inspiring others through education, ethics, and innovation. When I’m not coding or studying, I enjoy typing, learning new skills, and exploring the endless possibilities technology offers.
